🔍 The hidden finding:

The original report has 0 BUY signals with R:R ≥ 2.0 — every BUY call has avg R:R of just 0.61 (max 1.93). Meanwhile HOLDs average 0.94 and SELLs average 1.43.

This means the LLM is systematically giving BUY calls tighter targets than HOLD/SELL signals — which is the opposite of what you want. The 5% 严进 rule is forcing it to give BUY signals small upside targets ("buy on pullback to MA20") while HOLD signals get given multi-week upside targets like 52-week highs.

The real fix isn't the override — it's raising BUY targets. If you force BUY signals to project R:R ≥ 1.5 minimum, the override logic stops being needed.

💡 Recommendation — refined scoring logic

What I learned from 400 names:
  1. The R:R override works for the TSLA-type case (1 name — CEG), but most "good R:R" candidates are actually rangebound, not the broken-down winners we're trying to catch.
  2. The deeper issue is that BUY calls have systematically tight targets (avg R:R 0.61) vs SELL calls (1.43). The LLM treats BUY recommendations as "small scalps" and SELL as "real swing trade". That's backwards.
  3. The fix is two-fold:
    • (a) Raise BUY target floor — require BUY signals to project R:R ≥ 1.5 in the LLM prompt. If R:R < 1.5, downgrade to HOLD.
    • (b) Keep the override as a special case — if R:R ≥ 2.0 AND stock is below MA200 (extreme support), flip HOLD→BUY regardless of momentum score.
  4. If we apply rule (a) to today's report: ~62 of 82 BUY signals would downgrade to HOLD because their targets are too tight. That cleans up the noisy "small scalp" BUYs and leaves only BUY signals with real R:R.
  5. If we apply rule (b): 19 HOLDs flip to BUY, of which 1 (CEG) is the genuine "broken-down winner" TSLA pattern. The other 18 are rangebound setups that are decent but not special.
New scoring formula:
score = value × 0.05 + quality × 0.05 + momentum × 0.55 + order_flow × 0.15 + rr_score × 0.20
rr_score = min(100, max(0, R:R × 33)) // 1.5 R:R = 50 pts, 3.0+ R:R = 100 pts
signal: if R:R < 1.5 → HOLD regardless of score | if R:R >= 2.0 + below MA200 → override BUY
